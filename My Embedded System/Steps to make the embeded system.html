Steps to Create the Pressure Activated LED

  1. Get out your equipment
  2. Place the white end of the Connection Cable in the white part of the sensor
  3. Place a black jumper wire where the black wire is in the Connection wire is, a blue wire where the other blue wire is and a red wire where the other red wire is.
  4. Place the other end of the black jumper wire in the GND (Ground) section of the Arduino Uno
  5. Place the other end of the blue jumper wire in A0 spot of the Arduino UNO
  6. Place the other end of the red jumper wire in either the V5 or V3.3 in the Arduino UNO depending on how much voltage you want
  7. Now, get your LED and resistor and coil on of the resistors legs around the LED's longest leg.
  8. Place the resistors free leg in PIN 7 and place the shorter LED leg in the GND (Ground) section.
  9. Plug in the USB A into your computer and the USB type B into the Arduino UNO
  10. Load up the Arduino IDE and use the following code (Click Here)
  11. Verify the Code and Upload the Code to the Arduino UNO
  12. Press on the metal disk and if it goes above ten the LED will go off
  13. P.S. you can change the value of the by changing the 10 of [if (val > 10) { ] to what ever figure you want
